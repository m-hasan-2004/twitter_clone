About Twitter Clone

Twitter Clone is a microblogging platform that allows you to share short messages with your followers. Stay updated with the latest trends and interact with people across the globe.

Features

  • Follow and Unfollow Users
  • Block and Unblock Accounts
  • View Follower and Following Counts
  • Post, Edit, and Delete Tweets
  • Comment on Tweets, Edit, and Delete Comments
  • Sign Up, Sign In, and Manage Your Account
  • Delete Your Account

How to Start Using Twitter Clone

It's easy to get started with Twitter Clone. Sign up for a free account, follow users, and start tweeting. Customize your profile, and manage your privacy settings for a better experience.

  1. Sign Up or Sign In
  2. Update Your Profile
  3. Follow Interesting Accounts
  4. Start Tweeting!

User Interaction

Engage with your audience by replying to tweets, liking posts, and sharing content. Use the block feature to control who interacts with you. Manage your followers and stay connected with the community.

Account Management

Keep your account secure by regularly updating your password. Manage your account settings, privacy preferences, and personal information with ease. Delete your account anytime if you wish to leave the platform.
